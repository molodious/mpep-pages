Frequently Asked Questions

Are the problems similar to real FE exam problems?

Yes. Most problems are written to be as hard as actual exam problems or slightly harder. Every problem has at least one key insight embedded in it — often two or three.

Are solutions based on the Computer-Based Test (CBT) format?

Yes. All problems are solved using the NCEES FE Mechanical Reference Handbook PDF. Written explanations call out specific search terms to help you find what you need quickly.

Why is this subscription-based instead of a one-time purchase?

FE prep timelines vary a lot. Some students are ready in 6 weeks; others need 4–5 months. A subscription means you only pay for the time you actually use — no penalty for finishing early, no pressure to rush. If you need a break, cancel and re-enroll when you're ready.

Can I cancel my monthly subscription anytime?

Yes. No long-term commitment. Stay enrolled for as many or as few months as you need to prepare.

I've been out of school for a long time. Is this program right for me?

Yes. The fundamentals lectures give you the background and context you may have forgotten — or never fully grasped in college. It may take a bit more time and repetition, but the material is designed to work for everyone.

Is all the content available immediately?

Yes. All content is available to you the moment you enroll. No drip schedule, no waiting.

What if I have questions while studying?

The video and written solutions cover each problem in detail — most questions students have are answered there. If something's still unclear, Dan reviews questions submitted by students and addresses them directly, particularly those that come up often.

Are the videos and written solutions downloadable?

No. Access is through the learning platform and requires an internet connection.
